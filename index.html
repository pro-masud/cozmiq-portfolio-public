Your questions answered.

01How long does a typical project take to complete?

Project timelines vary with scope. Simple projects take 2–3 weeks; larger scopes 1–2 months. I’ll give an exact plan after discovery.

02Can you work with my existing brand and designs?

Absolutely. I extend your brand system and keep typography, color and tone consistent across all new assets.

03What makes your design process unique?

A tight, collaborative loop—discovery → wireframes → visual → prototype → test—with you in the loop at every step.

04Do you offer ongoing support after completion?

Yes. 30 days of complimentary tweaks & Q&A are included. Longer-term retainers are available.

05How do you handle confidentiality and IP rights?

All materials remain confidential. Final IP transfers to you on project completion as per our agreement.
